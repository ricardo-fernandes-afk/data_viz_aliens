Blink and
They‘re Gone?
UFO Sightings
by Duration

Most UFO encounters are over in a flash. Over 60% of sightings last less than 5 minutes, many even under a minute.

But duration depends on the type. “Triangles” and “spheres” tend to linger, while “lights” often vanish quickly.

Are these differences technical—or psychological?
We may never know, but the data points to something more than randomness.
